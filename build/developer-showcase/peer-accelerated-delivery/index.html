To see peer-accelerated streaming in action, use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox or Opera. In other browsers, you can still watch the video, but it will stream directly from the server.

Streamroot is a peer-accelerated streaming solution for online broadcasters. Combining traditional unicast delivery and P2P protocols, Streamroot offloads demand from servers by safely and securely connecting viewers watching streams simultaneously.
